## futuro simple

yo camino  => i walk
yo estoy caminando => i'm walking
yo camine => i walked

## estructura
will + verbo
yo caminare

i will wall

i will drink some water, please
yo bebere algo poco de agua por favor

## interrogativo
will you walk with my sister?
caminaras con mi hermana?

will you do some exercise?
haras algo de ejercicio?

i will get a book
conseguire un libro

goals => metas

i will get my goals
conseguire mis metas

este => this

conseguire mis metas este a;o
i will my goals this year

will you eat your vegetables?
tu comeras tus vegetales?

respuestas
no i will not => no no lo hare
yes i will => si si lo hare
not sure => no estoy seguro
maybe => tal vez
maybe later => tal vez mas tarde

ellos comeran pollo con mi hermano
they will eat chicken with my brother

i will run : I'll run
